01 / 07 · Welcome

The problem

Most people don't choose gravel.
They give up on the alternative.

Planting a garden sounds lovely — until you have to decide what, where, and whether any of it will survive. Faced with hundreds of choices, most people freeze. So they gravel it over, and feel a little guilty about it.

Sproutly removes the hard part: the deciding.

04 / 07 · The journey

What Sproutly does for you

It does the thinking, so you can just enjoy the result.

Knows your conditions

Looks up your local soil, rainfall and frost automatically — no homework required.

Picks plants that'll live

Quietly filters out anything that wouldn't survive your winter or your light.

Sized to your space

Tells you exactly how many of each plant to buy — no guesswork at the till.

Native-first

Prefers plants that belong in your local landscape and support the life around it.

Honest with you

Flags anything it's unsure about, and never promises more than it knows.

Yours to tweak

Add, remove or adjust anything before you buy. The plan is a starting point, not a rule.
